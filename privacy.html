Privacy Policy for Poll24

Effective Date: April 23, 2024

1. Introduction

Poll24 is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning data collection, use, and disclosure regarding users who interact with Poll24 without any requirement for personal information.

2. No Personal Information Collected

Poll24 does not collect any personal information from its users. Users can create and manage polls without the need for registration, providing a name, email address, or any other personal details.

3. No Cookies or Tracking Technologies

Poll24 does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

4. No Third-Party Services

Poll24 does not share data with any third-party services, nor does it integrate with third-party services that collect personal information from our users.

5. User Rights

Users who wish to manage or delete their polls can contact us directly at info@dcmademedia.com. While Poll24 does not collect personal information, we are committed to assist users with any concerns about their data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at info@dcmademedia.com.
